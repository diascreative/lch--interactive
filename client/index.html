POSITIVE ENERGY

A new kind of energy is lighting up schools, homes and businesses all over Oxfordshire: it’s renewable, locally-owned and developed for community benefit.

THE PEOPLE’S POWER STATION: Positive Energy in Real Time

The People’s Power Station is an online, live generation platform that allows us to show the contribution this ‘positive energy’ is making to Oxfordshire. To kick off the People’s Power Station, we have plugged in live generation data from:

  • The Low Carbon Hub community energy projects
  • Several community-owned energy projects such as Osney Lock Hydro and Oxford North Community Renewable Energy
  • Oxford City Council’s portfolio of renewable energy

As the project develops we’ll be plugging in more locally renewable energy generation to show the growing impact of local energy to help meet our energy needs. Our aim is to work with everyone across Oxfordshire to plug in all renewable generation across the County.

The project is still in development, so if you have any questions, feedback or suggestions, or would like your project to feature in the next phase, please email us at info@lowcarbonhub.org.uk

Of course, powering up is just part of the solution. We also need to be working together to drive down our energy use – so that it takes less energy to power Oxfordshire. As we get more efficient, the energy need reduces, so the energy generated by the people’s power station will power more people. So, whether an energy generator or energy user, we are all part of the People’s Power Station.

About the Low Carbon Hub

The Low Carbon Hub is an award-winning social enterprise, working for a massive change in the UK's energy system. We think we need to get much more local about our energy system, and much more renewable, so that the benefits of renewable energy generation strengthen local communities.  We develop community-owned renewable energy in Oxfordshire and re-invest 100% of our own surplus in our mission to create an energy system we can all feel good about.
